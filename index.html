About Us

Mission

We pioneer sustainable geotechnical solutions by integrating circular economy principles to reduce waste and carbon footprint in construction and infrastructure development.

Vision

A world where geotechnical engineering operates in complete harmony with Earth's ecosystems, promoting sustainability through innovative material reuse and low-impact techniques.

Research Themes

Sustainable Geotechnics

Developing eco-friendly soil stabilization methods using industrial byproducts and recycled materials.

Soil Mechanics Foundation Design

Circular Solutions

Transforming construction waste into valuable geotechnical materials through innovative processing techniques.

Material Science Waste Valorization

Green Infrastructure

Designing low-carbon geotechnical systems with minimized environmental impact throughout their lifecycle.

LCA Eco-Design

Soil Contamination and Remediation

Exploring sustainable approaches to remediate contaminated soil, utilizing advanced bioremediation techniques and eco-friendly materials.

New Research Grant Awarded

April 6, 2025

The GeoCircular Research Group has been awarded a significant research grant to study the application of recycled polymers in soil stabilization. This project aims to reduce plastic waste and improve soil quality for construction purposes.

Dr. Nikkhah Nasab Presents at International Conference

Dr. Saeid Nikkhah Nasab presented his latest findings on sustainable foundation design at the International Geotechnical Engineering Conference in Rome. His presentation highlighted innovative methods for reducing carbon emissions in urban infrastructure projects.
