About Us

GeoCircular is a multidisciplinary research group focused on sustainable remediation of contaminated soils and critical raw materials recovery through geotechnical innovation and circular economy principles.

Our Projects

We work on a wide range of projects from geoenvironmental modeling to innovative reuse of industrial by-products in soil remediation and construction.

Our Team

Our team consists of researchers, engineers, and environmental scientists collaborating across disciplines and institutions worldwide.
